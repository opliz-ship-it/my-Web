The Developer Behind
The Terminal

I'm Mohamed, known online as Opliz — a Computer Science student at the Faculty of Computers, Information, and Artificial Intelligence (RST), driven by a relentless curiosity for how systems work under the hood.

My world lives at the intersection of two disciplines: the rigid logic of back-end architecture — where databases breathe, APIs connect, and servers never sleep — and the meticulous art of product photography, where light, composition, and storytelling turn objects into experiences.

Currently deep-diving into ERD design, database relationships, and server-side logic, I'm building a foundation to create intelligent, scalable platforms that solve real-world problems — from AI-powered pharmacies to industrial digital presences.
